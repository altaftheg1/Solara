CHANGING FUTURES, ONE GIRL AT A TIME

This platform exists to help reduce gender inequality in Uttar Pradesh by giving people a safe space to express their experiences and be heard. Many voices go unheard due to fear or pressure, and this space is built to change that by creating awareness through real stories and honest conversations.

THE VOICES WE SUPPORT

Solara is for every woman and girl in Uttar Pradesh who has felt unheard. For survivors, for dreamers, for those still searching for the courage to speak. And for the people who stand beside them — families, educators, and advocates who believe every voice matters.

HOW CHANGE HAPPENS

Change starts with a single story shared. When one voice speaks, others follow. Solara turns individual experiences into a collective movement — through anonymous posts, community chats, and shared videos that spread awareness across UP and beyond.

UNDERSTANDING UTTAR PRADESH

Uttar Pradesh is India's most populous state — home to over 240 million people. It is a land of deep culture, ancient history, and resilient communities. But beneath its richness lies a reality that many of its daughters live every day.

The Culture

UP is home to the Taj Mahal, the holy city of Varanasi, and some of India's most celebrated festivals. Its culture values community, family, and tradition — but for many women, those same values can limit their freedom and voice.

The Reality

UP has one of the lowest female literacy rates in India. Child marriage, gender-based violence, and lack of access to education remain widespread issues. Many girls are expected to marry young and remain silent about their experiences.

Why It Matters

When women are given a platform to speak, things change. Awareness leads to action. Solara exists to be that platform — a safe, anonymous space where voices from UP and beyond can finally be heard without fear.
