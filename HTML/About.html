ABOUT SOLARA

A platform built for the unheard. A movement for change.

What is SOLARA?

SOLARA is a safe, anonymous online platform dedicated to reducing gender inequality in Uttar Pradesh, India. It gives women and girls a space to share their stories, connect with others, and access support — without fear of judgment or exposure.

The name SOLARA comes from the word "solar" — light. Because every voice shared here is a beam of light cutting through silence.

How We Help the Community

Safe Conversations

A global chat where women can talk openly, ask questions, and find community — all anonymously.

Real Stories

A stories page where members post their real experiences. Reading one story can give another person the courage to speak.

Videos & Documentaries

Curated videos, interviews, and documentaries about women's lives across UP and India.

Awareness

By aggregating voices in one place, SOLARA turns individual pain into collective awareness that reaches beyond borders.

Made by UnitM

SOLARA was created by UnitM — a student-led initiative with a simple belief: that technology should be used to amplify the voices of those who have been silenced.

This platform was built as part of a project to address real social issues through digital tools. Every design decision, every feature, and every word on this site was chosen with the women of Uttar Pradesh in mind.

SOLARA is not a business. It is a statement.

Emergency Protocols & Safety

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, please contact emergency services first. SOLARA also has the following protocols in place to keep our community safe.

If You Are in Danger Right Now

Call 112 (India's national emergency number) immediately.

Women's helpline: 1091

Child helpline: 1098

Domestic violence: 181

Signs You May Need Support

You feel unsafe at home or outside.

You are being pressured into something against your will.

You feel persistently sad, hopeless, or worthless.

You have experienced or witnessed violence.

You have thoughts of harming yourself.

How SOLARA Keeps You Safe

All usernames are anonymous — no real names required.

No email or phone number is collected.

Posts that contain threats or harmful content can be reported.

We do not share your data with anyone.
