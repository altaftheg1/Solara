Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information SOLARA collects, how it is used, and how we protect it. SOLARA is a student-created project and is not a commercial service.

2. What Information We Collect

SOLARA collects very little information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your real name
  • We do not collect your email address
  • We do not collect your phone number
  • We do not collect your location

The only thing stored is your chosen anonymous username, which is saved in your browser's local storage. This never leaves your device.

3. How We Use Information

The anonymous username stored in your browser is only used to display your name next to messages and posts within the platform. It is not sent to any server or shared with anyone.

4. Local Storage

SOLARA uses your browser's local storage to save your username and any messages you send. Local storage only exists on your own device — it is not stored on any external server. Clearing your browser data will remove everything.

5. Third Party Content

SOLARA embeds YouTube videos on the Videos page. These videos are hosted by YouTube (Google) and their own privacy policy applies when you watch them. We do not control what YouTube collects.

6. Children's Privacy

SOLARA does not knowingly collect any information from children. The platform is designed to be safe and anonymous for all users including young people.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will appear on this page. We encourage you to check back occasionally.

8. Contact
